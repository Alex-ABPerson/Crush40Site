What is Crush 40?

Crush 40 is a hard-rock focused musical group.

Most commonly associated with writing vocal themes for the Sonic the Hedgehog series.

Drums and bass?

"Crush 40" does not have a set drummer and bass player!

They either rely on a backing track live, or some other member to fill in temporarily.

Why "Crush 40"?

The band was originally titled "Sons of Angels" - but they soon discovered a Norwegian rock band already had the same name, who were thinking "Sons of...".

So, they changed it - and their new name, "Crush 40", was a combination of Johnny's wishes at the time, as well as Jun's personal preferences:

What is Crush 40?

Crush 40 is a Japanese-American band that produces high-quality, hard rock music.

The lead vocalist is Johnny Gioeli and the lead guitarist is Jun Senoue.

Johnny Gioeli

Jun Senoue

They are most known as the band behind many of the Sonic the Hedgehog video game songs.

However, with time they have made their own personal songs, such as their Rise Again EP or 2 Nights 2 Remember album.
