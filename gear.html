Guitars

By far the most prominent set of gear for Crush 40 is Jun's guitars.

Jun uses custom-made ESP Guitars for his own works and Crush 40. The guitars have different designs on them - some being used for general Sonic/Shadow songs, and some being used for very precise game soundtracks.

Jun most commonly plays the ESP GL "SONIC", ESP SONIC-II and ESP SHADOW-II at most Crush 40 shows, but at some shows he also brings the dedicated game guitars to play.

He's been in a sponsorship deal with ESP ever since he first worked on Sonic Adventure - and as such is required to use these guitars at his shows, but that's not a problem, because they're awesome and he always sounds great anyway!

Amplifiers

Jun owns and typically uses one of these four amps for most of his work, including his work with Crush 40.

  • Soldano SLO-100R

    This, alongside the Dual Rectifier, is an amp Jun commonly uses at Crush 40 shows. Many of his tracks utilise this amp.

  • MESA Dual Rectifier

    Jun has used this amp at various shows. This amp was heavily used in Shadow the Hedgehog to achieve the darker and deeper tones.

  • Suhr Badger 18

    One song this amp was used for is Is It You, from Super Sonic Songs.

  • Peavey 5150

    Jun also owns this amp and uses it occasionally.

Cabinet

Jun typically uses a Soldano 4x12" cabinet in combination with one of his amps.

Virtual

While Jun usually uses hardware, he has used some software-based equipment in his work!

Jun uses Line 6 PODs on some of the most iconic Crush 40 songs, and has used some other equipment live!

  • Line 6 Pod PRO

    Jun used this for many Sonic Adventure 2 tracks - most notably for the iconic and hit song Live & Learn!

    It's possible to more or less replicate the guitar tone on the song with one of these!
